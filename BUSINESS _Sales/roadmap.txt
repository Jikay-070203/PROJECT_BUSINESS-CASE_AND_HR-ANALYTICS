📌 Project Summary: Business Case - Sales

🔹 Project Goals  
- Analyze sales data to make smarter business decisions.  
- Optimize marketing strategies & product management.  

🎯 Specific Objectives
- Clean and prepare sales data.  
- Analyze sales trends over time.  
- Forecast future revenue.  
- Identify key factors influencing revenue.  

🔍 Implementation Steps

1️⃣ Data Preparation:
- Load data from Google Drive.  
- Read data from CSV & inspect information.  
- Clean data: handle missing values, remove erroneous data.  

2️⃣ Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA):
- Display descriptive statistics.  
- Visualize sales trends over time, product categories, and sales regions.  
- Analyze correlations between factors.  

3️⃣ Sales Prediction Model:
- Use Linear Regression or Decision Tree models for revenue prediction.  
- Evaluate model performance using MAE, RMSE metrics.  

4️⃣ Conclusion & Recommendations:
- Identify key factors impacting revenue.  
- Provide actionable insights to improve business strategies.  

🛠 Libraries Used
- `pandas`, `numpy`: Data processing.  
- `matplotlib`, `seaborn`: Data visualization.  
- `sklearn`: Sales prediction modeling.
